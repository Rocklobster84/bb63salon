About

Established in 2010, we have been a staple salon in Fort Worth Texas for many years. Originally we opened a location on Bluebonnet Circle near TCU, then added another near the historic Fort Worth Stockyards. After taking a bit of time away to focus on family, we are pleased to announce that in October 2022 we reopened one single location in the historic Ridglea Theater strip, at 3309 Winthrop Ave., Suite 67, Fort Worth, TX 76116. We are located on the historic Camp Bowie Boulevard right next to the Ridglea Room. As lovers of local talent and all things music, we have always supported the Fort Worth Rock Assembly, and now we are happy to say that we share a building with three incredible music venues. Because of that however, we do have limited openings in the evenings. Although we love the sound of a great live concert, we still want to provide our clients with the best atmosphere possible for a relaxing service. At BB 63 Salon, you will find some of the best hairstylists and the most amazing estheticians in town. Each member of our salon aims to please and takes great pride in satisfying each and every guest. Please review all of our services and let us help you to feel beautiful inside and out. Consider scheduling an aesthetic service, either before or after your hair service, and we will do our best to accommodate! We look forward to hearing from you soon!

Our Location

Our new location near Camp Bowie and I-30 completes the whole package in the heart of central Fort Worth, where great dining, shopping, and entertainment can be found a stone's throw away. So, whether you are headed off to a special night in the Cultural District nearby or just want to freshen up your everyday look, BB 63 Salon is your one-stop-shop to celebrate and refresh your own unique beauty and style.
