About

BB 63 Salon has been a staple in the DFW Metroplex for more than 5 years. Offering a wide range of services from unique haircuts, dyes, and special event styling to waxing services. Friendly and dedicated service while making your hair fantasies come true is just one of the reasons our guests return to us again and again.

Our Stylists

Each stylist is an independent business owner while working with BB 63. Talented and creative, commanding their own individual style. Our stylists are highly trained with years of experience! It is assured that you will leave here happy and in love with your new look!

Our Location

Our new location near Camp Bowie and I-30 completes the whole package in the heart of central Fort Worth, where great dining, shopping, and entertainment can be found a ston'es throw away. So, whether you are headed off to a special night in the Cultural District nearby or just want to freshen up your everyday look, BB63 Salon is your one-stop-shop to celebrate and refresh your own unique beauty and style.
